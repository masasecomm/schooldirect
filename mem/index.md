# Project Memory

## Core
All site copy follows the "Dynamic Trust" framework: SME voice, declarative, no fluff, no hedging, no "in today's world" intros.
Write in South African second-language English. Plain words, short sentences. No big words.
Never use invisible Unicode: no em dash, en dash, curly quotes, non-breaking spaces, or zero-width chars. Straight quotes and hyphens only.
Lead with the answer in the first sentence of every section. One idea per paragraph. Use H1/H2/H3, bullets, and tables for extractability.
Anchor authority with: Department of Basic Education, Provincial Education Departments, EMIS, ISASA, SGB, Section 21, Quintile system, NSC.
Surface first-party signals: our 3,082-school dataset, district coverage, walk-in centres, EMIS-level detail.
Frame freshness to 2026 where relevant.
